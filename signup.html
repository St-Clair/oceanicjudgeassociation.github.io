Become a Judge

In order to become a certified OJA Judge you must:

  • Read and agree to the Constitution:
  • Read and agree to the Code of Conduct:
  • Email info@oceanicjudgeassociation.org that you accept to be bound by these documents, including your Full Legal Name on the email
  • Pay Dues ($25 Annually, renewing on the 1st January each year) to the bank details provided by return email
  • Wait for your application to be accepted by the OJA Board
  • Either transfer your level from Judge Academy or:
    • Complete the L1 Rules/Policy Test
    • Complete the EM1 Certification

    More information on the certification process can be found here: Level Definition Document
